How to make this thick Carrot Soup?

Ingredients (serves 2)

  • Carrots - (2)
  • Onion - (1/2)
  • Milk - 5TBSP
  • Salt - 1tsp or to taste
  • Pepper - 1tsp or t taste
  • Sugar - 1tsp or to taste
  • Dried Oregano - 1 tsp
  • Olive Oil - 2TBSP

Procedure

  • Chop carrot into big pieces and mince onions into tiny piece.
  • Saut√© them together in 1 TBSP hot olive oil until well-browned.Cool.
  • Grind the roasted mix without adding any water.
  • Heat 1TBSP olive oil, roast oregano, pepper and salt in it.
  • Add thick carrot paste into hot seasoning. Add 1 cup of water and sugar and let it boil for five minutes. Adjust consistency as necessary.
  • Serve hot. Warm ciabatta or foccaccia bread taste very good with this thick dinner soup.
